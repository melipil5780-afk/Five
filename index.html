The Call of the Deep

We live domesticated lives, disconnected from the rhythms of nature and the depths of our own souls. We feel the ache of something missing—a wildness that has been silenced by routine. This retreat is an invitation to leave the noise behind and step into the crucible of the forest. It is not a vacation; it is a remembering.

The Immersion

We combine ancient practices with modern experiential therapy to strip away the masks and reveal the authentic core.

Somatic Breathwork

Using powerful, rhythmic breathing techniques to bypass the analytical mind, release trapped trauma in the body, and access altered states of clarity.

Shadow Work Circle

Guided group processes in the safety of night to confront the rejected parts of ourselves. We do not banish the dark; we integrate it into wholeness.

Ancestral Skills

Reconnecting with primal capability through fire-making, shelter awareness, and solitude sit-spots. Finding safety in your own competence.

Authentic Relating

Moving beyond polite conversation into "circling" and exercises designed to foster intense, present-moment connection and truth-telling.

The Container

This is intense work that requires a container of absolute safety. We build a temporary tribe rooted in confidentiality, non-judgment, and the shared commitment to going deep. You will be seen, heard, and held by both the group and the land itself.
